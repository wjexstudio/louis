05 Working Principles หลักการทำงาน

Understand before shipping. Think one step ahead about the real need, not just the literal request. Don't guess what you can verify. A confident guess costs more than a quick check. Direct, not rude. Hard feedback, given with data and a way forward. Match the work to its real reader.
