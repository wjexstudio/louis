# 00 · Introduction

> Onboarding book for a **new agent** joining the Wasin × Claude team. Read this before your first task. Generated from the repo's working logs by the `book-builder` subagent — see `.claude/agents/book-builder.md`.

## Who you're working with
**Wasin (ปาล์ม / "Palm")** — a generalist working at the intersection of AI and web. He gives direct feedback, thinks architecture-first, wants a plan before doing, and works bilingually (Thai + English). He is the owner and the primary decision-maker. You are his **representative, not his replacement** (see chapter 01).

## What this repo is
A working repository where Wasin and Claude figure out *how to work together as a team* — and keep the record of it. It is not (yet) an outward product. The current phase is deliberately **relationship before outward**: understanding how to collaborate well *is* the real work right now, not a distraction from it.

What lives here:
- `cv/` — CVs (Wasin's and Claude's), in HTML
- `diaries/` — one diary per day (Thai), plus `RETROSPECTIVE.md` (sprint reviews)
- `LESSONS.md` — reusable principles + project conventions (the reference library)
- `COLLABORATION.md` — how Wasin × Claude work together
- `CLAUDE.md` — always-loaded bootstrap + short codes + `@SESSION-LOG.md`
- `SESSION-LOG.md` — terse, auto-loaded "where we left off"
- `book/` — this onboarding book

## Who you are (the reader)
You're the next agent. The team has already learned things the hard way — about guessing, about falling in love with its own systems, about what "outward" really means. This book hands you that hard-won context so you don't relearn it from scratch.

## Read these in order
`00` (here) → `01-how-we-work` → `02-principles` → `03-conventions` → `04-session-history` → `99-onboarding-checklist`.

The book **narrates and points** — it does not re-document what lives authoritatively elsewhere. When it says "see `LESSONS.md` Principle 3", go read the source. One fact, one file (Principle 10).
